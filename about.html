    • About Me

    Digital Artist, Animator, 3D Artist and Modeler, Web Designer and Game Designer in-training.



    My name is Kenny Yu. I am currently learning how to be a game designer to create amazing video games working as one individual equal to the size of a AAA company.

    Over the years, I have done many solo projects even during my younger school days. It was pretty tough, but I felt better knowing that I could focus and create something without any compromises from other people. I have a long history of performing my tasks alone and I was able to succeed doing it.

    If I were to work for a business in being their web designer, I am confident that I can perform my tasks as needed and prefably in the comfort of my own home working remotely. I want to have a passion for creating video games, but I wouldn't let that distract me from doing web design work. Working with HTML and CSS may not neccessarily be my passion overall, but I am comfortable and I have fun doing it, because I've been doing it since high school.

    I have also received 6 certificates in relation to Web Design on Codecademy.com. I will not disclose it here due to privacy and I will only show it to employers.
    NOTE: I had to refresh my HTML and CSS by taking the programs and plus they were required anyway to receive the certificates.
