    How it's done:

    In the image slider down below, click the arrows to view each step in the process of how I create these 3D models.


    Steps in Number Order explained.

    1. The first step is me modeling your character. This is a long process and it takes a lot of time to get each angle and part correctly.
      WIPs (Work in Progress) are available

    2. The second step is when I bring your model to another software that decides the scale of your model that will be printed. After that, the 3D model will print. This can sometimes take more than one day.

    3. The third step is when I take off all the supports that hold the model together. After that, the model will need a primer spray applied. The primer spray is required if you want paint to stay on the model. The primer spray must dry.

    4. The final step begins after the primer is dried. Then I begin painting. After the paint, a clear coat primer is applied to keep the paint in place.
